# Notion-customer-customer-success-case-study
Customer Success case study analyzing Notion's onboarding journey to identify UX friction, improve customer activation, and increase product adoption.

## Overview

This project is a Customer Success case study that evaluates the onboarding experience of first-time Notion users. The objective is to identify friction points, analyze customer behavior throughout the onboarding journey, and recommend actionable improvements to enhance user activation, engagement, and retention.

---

## Project Objectives

- Analyze the end-to-end onboarding journey.
- Identify customer pain points and friction.
- Evaluate onboarding experience from a Customer Success perspective.
- Recommend improvements to increase activation and product adoption.

---

## Deliverables

- Customer Persona
- Customer Journey Map

- Pain Point Analysis
- Improvement Opportunities

---

## Methodology

- Customer Journey Mapping
- User Research
- UX Analysis
- Customer Success Framework
- Product Thinking

---

## Key Findings

- New users experience information overload during onboarding.
- Limited contextual guidance affects feature discoverability.
- AI workspace generation lacks sufficient explanation.
- Desktop app recommendation interrupts the onboarding flow.

---

## Recommendations

- Introduce progressive onboarding.
- Improve contextual guidance.
- Enhance AI feature explanations.
- Optimize feature discovery.
- Simplify onboarding communication.
